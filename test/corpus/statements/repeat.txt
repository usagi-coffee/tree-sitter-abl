  DISPLAY x.
END.

  x = x + 1.
END.

REPEAT UNTIL done:
  DISPLAY done.
END.

main: REPEAT ON STOP UNDO, RETURN ERROR NEW Progress.Lang.AppError("x", 1) ON ERROR UNDO, THROW:
  LEAVE.
END.
